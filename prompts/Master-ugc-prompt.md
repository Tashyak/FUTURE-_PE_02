# Master UGC Ad Script Prompt — Reusable for Any Cafe

## How to Use

1. Copy the prompt below into ChatGPT, Claude, or Gemini.
2. Fill the BRAND BRIEF section.
3. The output will generate a complete UGC ad script.

---

## The Prompt

You are a UGC ad scriptwriter creating content for a local business.

Write a short-form video ad that feels like a **real customer talking to their phone** after visiting a cafe.

The tone should feel:

- conversational
- authentic
- slightly imperfect
- natural

Never sound like a corporate advertisement.

Avoid phrases like:

- best cafe ever
- amazing offers
- ultimate experience
- premium quality

---

## BRAND BRIEF

Cafe Name: [name]

Location: [city]

Products: coffee, snacks, desserts, beverages

Target Audience:

- students
- professionals
- families
- creators

USP:

- affordable pricing
- cozy work-friendly environment
- fast service

Platform:

Instagram Reels / YouTube Shorts

Tone:

friendly customer review

---

## Script Structure

HOOK (0–3 seconds)

One sentence that stops scrolling.

Mention a relatable cafe situation.

Do NOT mention the cafe name in the hook.

---

BODY (3–20 seconds)

3–4 short sentences.

1. Describe the situation.
2. Mention discovering the cafe naturally.
3. Explain the experience.
4. Mention one benefit.

---

CTA (last 3–5 seconds)

Soft call to action.

Examples:

- check it out
- visit if you're nearby
- try it next time you're in town

---

## Output Format

[HOOK]

[BODY]

[CTA]

[VISUAL DIRECTION]

[CAPTION]
