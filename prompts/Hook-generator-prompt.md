# Hook Generator Prompt

Generate 12 scroll-stopping hooks for a cafe advertisement.

Hooks must:

- be under 12 words
- feel natural
- sound like a real person speaking
- create curiosity

Do NOT mention the cafe name.

---

## Prompt

You are a short-form content strategist.

Create 12 hooks for a cafe located in Kakinada.

Organize them into:

Pain Hooks  
Discovery Hooks  
Story Hooks  
Identity Hooks

Each hook should feel like a real customer sharing an experience.
