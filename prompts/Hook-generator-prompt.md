# Hook Generator Prompt

Generate 12 scroll-stopping hooks for a cafe advertisement.

Hooks must:

- be under 12 words
- feel natural
- sound like a real person speaking
- create curiosity

Do NOT mention the cafe name.

---

## Prompt

You are a short-form content strategist.

Create 12 hooks for a cafe located in Kakinada.

Organize them into:

Pain Hooks  
Discovery Hooks  
Story Hooks  
Identity Hooks

Each hook should feel like a real customer sharing an experience.

# Hook Bank — 12 Scroll-Stopping Hooks

Pain Hooks
(Name a frustration people experience when looking for a cafe)

"I just wanted coffee and a quiet place to sit."
"Every cafe I tried was either noisy or overpriced."
"I couldn't find a good cafe to work from."
"I needed coffee and WiFi without the chaos."

---

Discovery Hooks
(Moment of finding something unexpectedly good)

"I accidentally found the coziest cafe in Kakinada."
"I walked in for coffee… stayed for two hours."
"This cafe surprised me more than I expected."

---

Story Hooks
(Drop viewers directly into a moment)

"So I walked into this cafe just to grab coffee…"
"I came here for five minutes and stayed an hour."
"I wasn't planning to sit down, but the vibe changed that."

---

Identity Hooks
(Call out a specific type of viewer)

"If you're a student looking for a study cafe…"
"If you love coffee and quiet places…"
"If you need a chill place to work from…"
