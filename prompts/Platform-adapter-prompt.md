# Platform Adapter Prompt

This prompt adapts a single UGC script so it fits naturally on different short-form platforms.

Different platforms reward slightly different styles of storytelling and pacing.

This prompt helps adjust the script for:

- Instagram Reels
- YouTube Shorts

while keeping the **authentic UGC tone**.

---

## How to Use

1. Copy this prompt into ChatGPT, Claude, or Gemini.
2. Paste your script into the **SCRIPT section**.
3. The AI will generate two versions:
   - Instagram Reels version
   - YouTube Shorts version

---

## The Prompt

You are a short-form video strategist.

Your task is to adapt a UGC-style cafe ad script for different platforms while keeping the tone authentic and conversational.

The video should feel like a **real customer talking to their phone after visiting a cafe**.

Avoid corporate language and marketing buzzwords.

---

## Business Context

Business: Local Cafe  
Location: Kakinada  

Target Audience:

- Students
- Professionals
- Families
- Coffee lovers
- Creators working remotely

USP:

- Affordable pricing
- Cozy work-friendly environment
- Fast service

## Adaptation Rules

### Instagram Reels Version

- More casual and friendly
- Faster pacing
- Shorter sentences
- Feels like a spontaneous recommendation
- Can include engagement cues like:
  - "save this place"
  - "tag your coffee buddy"

---

### YouTube Shorts Version

- Slightly more descriptive
- Clear storytelling
- Slightly longer explanation allowed
- Focus on sharing a quick personal experience
